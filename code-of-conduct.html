Code of Conduct

Importance

We work hard to make a safe and welcoming environment for everyone in the AWS community. This Code of Conduct explains the conduct required from all AWS community members and event participants. This Code of Conduct is non-negotiable. Any behavior that runs counter to this Code of Conduct will not be tolerated.

Scope

All community members and event participants (including employees, attendees, vendors, sponsors, speakers, volunteers, and guests) must comply with this Code of Conduct. This Code of Conduct applies to the main event and all related events, physical, online or otherwise.

Behavior

All community members and event participants are expected to support a safe and supportive environment within all AWS community events and programs.

Thus, all members and participants agree to comply with all applicable laws, all community event-specific requirements, venue-specific policies, and health and safety procedures.

Dress Code

Participants and attendees in all physical events are required to be in smart casual, business casual, or business attire. Persons who wear slippers, shorts, and other inappropriate attire may be denied entry in event venues, regardless of ticket or participation status.

Prohibited Acts

>

All community members and event participants agree that they will:

  • Not engage in any disruptive speech or behavior;
  • Not interfere with the event or another individual's participation in the event;
  • Not interfere with the operation of community events, programs, blogs, online forums, and social media platforms;
  • Not attempt to receive benefits that they are not entitled to at AWS community events and programs, and across blogs, online forums, and social media platforms;
  • Not attend or participate while drunk or intoxicated;
  • Not bring into any event any harmful substance or object;
  • Not engage in any form of harassment, offensive, discriminatory, or threatening speech or behavior, including but not limited to, relating to race, gender, gender identity and expression, national origin, religion, disability, marital status, age, sexual orientation, military or veteran status, or other protected category.

Consequences

Any member or event participant not complying with this Code of Conduct may be denied entry or required to leave from any and all events, which AWS Community Day PH 2023 Volunteer organizers may determine at their sole discretion.

There will be no refund of any fees in case of any denial of entry or demand to leave any event.

Any violation of this Conduct of Conduct by any member or event participant may result in their disqualification from future AWS community events.

AWS Community Day PH 2023 volunteers and organizers may contact local law enforcement whenever they deem necessary.

Contact

If you have any concern regarding this Code of Conduct, please promptly contact AWS User Group Philippines organizers at info@awsug.ph
