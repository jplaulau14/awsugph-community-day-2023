    WHO ARE WE?

    AWS User Group Philippines (AWSUG.PH) is a Philippine-based community united by its members' passion for Amazon Web Services and its related tools and technologies, including cloud computing, design implementation and servicing, high scalability, performance computing, production use cases, blockchain, Artificial Intelligence, and much more.

    AWS Community Days are community-organized cloud education events that feature technical discussions and demos by expert AWS users and industry leaders from around the world.

    Community-based learning is the best venue for enthusiasts to connect, explore and discuss the concepts by sharing first-hand experiences and familiar, applicable solutions.

    Everyone is welcome to learn, share, and network!

           

    What to expect from AWS Community Day - Roadshow?

    • Discover and share how AWS changes lives.
    • Know more about industry trends, nuances, and innovations.
    • Connect with 100+ AWS users, DevOps, developers, and solutions architects.
    • Network with fellow AWS enthusiasts.
    • Renew your excitement about the important work that you do.
    • Gain more visibility and publicity for your organization.
    • Learn and bring valuable information for your business growth.
    • Win AWS goodies!
